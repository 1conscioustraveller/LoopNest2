About LoopNest

LoopNest started in a Sheffield bedroom in 2021 after a broken audio interface left the founder with nothing but stock drums and a web browser. The goal: make the fastest, lightest sketchpad for rhythm ideas. Everything else—log-ins, paywalls, 3-gigabyte sample packs—got stripped away.

We stay independent, funded by small donations and sticker sales. No investors, no data mining, no dark patterns. Just loops.
